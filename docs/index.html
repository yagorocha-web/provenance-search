Why it matters
The problem

The art market is one of the world's largest unregulated asset classes.

Ownership history is scattered across museum records, sale catalogues, and registries that rarely talk to each other.

The signal

Custody gaps, missing records, valuation anomalies.

A decade with no documented owner. A price with no comparable. Each is reported as an explicit, sourced fact — not smoothed over.

The passport

A cited record with a transparent score.

The confidence score is computed by a fixed, published rule set from the facts gathered — never guessed by the AI.
